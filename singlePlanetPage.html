MUSTAFAR

A small planet located in the outer rim that is famous for its volcanic lava sea and molten rock plains. Mustafar's lava flows are mined for unique and valuable minerals that are used in the assembly of battle droids. Come visit the location where Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi defeated the newly appointed Sith Lord Darth Vader.
